FREECYCLE



MAKE OFFERS. PUBLISH NEEDS. DONATE. FEEL GOOD.

The Freecycle platform is primarily intended to recycle common household stuff for free to underprivileged recipients, though anyone in need can use the platform. It intends to help BPL people with urgent medical supplies, books for children, food for street children and other support through community involvement.
Features: Browse Offers by City or Distance from you current location. View local emergencies. View common household and other needs. Create Offers, emergencies and needs. Subscribe to events like urgent medical needs and other emergencies or for items of interest. e.g if you need a phone,subscribe to event categiry electronics to get notified of offers made.
